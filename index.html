EzChain – Blockchain solution for Fintech, logistic and ecommerce.

Our vision is to create and design the most effective and suitable blockchain for daily transaction, more secure, transparent but free for everyone.

EzChain is based on Ethereum but it can solve its scalability problem. On EzChain, user can enjoin 2 outstanding features: zero transfer fee and 2 seconds transaction time.

Via its smart contracts, EzChain guarantees the security.

Based on Ethereum, EzChain supports all EVM compatible smart-contracts, protocols, atomic cross-chain token transfer. In the near future, when sharding, child-chain and private chain are applied to EzChain, the speed of transaction will be reduced more, the number of transaction per second will increase but the fee will still stay completely zero; EzChain will become the most ideal blockchain for building dApps, token issuances, token integration.
